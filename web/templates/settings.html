Settings

Configuration is stored in the database and used by every run.

Delivery schedule (evening send)

Once a day the digest is finalized and emailed / sent to Telegram. When collection (below) is enabled, this just sends what was collected; otherwise it scrapes and sends in one go.

Collection schedule (scrape new tweets)

Scrape the newest tweets into the archive every few hours so the digest builds up over the day. Already-seen tweets are skipped, so frequent runs stay light.

Processing schedule (live draft refresh)

Rebuild the “Today” draft digest (filter, thread, cluster, summarize) every few hours so the portal shows the day so far. Renders only — nothing is emailed until the evening delivery.
